What problem does Rodevo solve?

Current state

The contemporary market for private instruction is often characterized by fragmentation and a lack of transparency. Both students and tutors frequently encounter significant barriers, ranging from inefficient scheduling systems to poor vetting processes. These logistical challenges often detract from the primary objective: the effective delivery of high-quality education.

Rodevo’s system

Rodevo provides a centralized, professional environment engineered to eliminate these inefficiencies. By implementing a standard for platform interactions and optimizing the logistical aspects of tutoring, we allow users to focus exclusively on academic development. Our platform serves as a reliable conduit for those who demand precision and professional integrity in their education.

Features & benefits

Matching Algorithm: Our system utilizes comprehensive criteria to ensure that learners are paired with educators whose expertise and instructional methods align precisely with their requirements.
Unified Instructional Suite: Rodevo integrates secure communication, document sharing, and scheduling into a singular, professional interface, ensuring that all educational engagements are conducted within a stable and efficient environment.
Administrative Optimization: For the professional educator, Rodevo offers a rich suite of tools designed to manage student relations, billing, and session organization, therefore reducing the burden of manual administration.
Verified Academic Integrity: We maintain a commitment to transparency. Through verified credentials and structured performance, we ensure that the platform remains a trusted resource for elite academic support.
Privacy Policy
Updated: January 2026.


1. Introduction
This Privacy Policy outlines how Rodevo collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit our waitlist website and sign up for early access. By providing your email address, you agree to the terms described below.

2. Information We Collect
To keep our process simple and secure, we only collect the following:
Email Address: This is the primary information we require to add you to the waitlist.
Usage Data: We may collect basic technical data through standard web logs to help us maintain the website’s security and performance.

3. How We Use Your Information
Your information is used strictly for the following purposes:
To notify you of your position on the waitlist.
To send you updates regarding Rodevo’s development and launch milestones.
To provide an invitation link when the platform becomes available.
To improve our marketing efforts as we introduce Rodevo to a wider audience.

4. Third-Party Services
We use Formspree to process our sign-up forms. When you submit your email, it is securely transmitted to Formspree for storage and management. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third parties for their marketing purposes.

5. Cookies and Tracking
Since we advertise on social media platforms, our website may use "cookies" or tracking pixels. These tools help us understand which advertisements brought you to our site and allow us to measure the effectiveness of our outreach. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time without losing access to the waitlist form.

6. Data Security
We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your email address from unauthorized access. While no method of digital transmission is 100% secure, we take all reasonable precautions to safeguard the data you entrust to us.

7. Your Rights and Choices
You have full control over your information:
Unsubscribe: Every update email we send will contain an "Unsubscribe" link.
Data Deletion: If you wish to be removed from our waitlist entirely, you may contact us, and we will delete your record from our database.

8. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy as we move closer to our official launch. Any significant changes will be communicated via the email address you provided.

9. Contact Us
If you have questions regarding this policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us through our official contact page.
